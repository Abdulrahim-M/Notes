Power typical requirements

1. Tractors

Conventional Diesel Tractors
  • Small Utility Tractors: 2060 hp (1545 kW)
  • Mid-size Row Crop Tractors: 100200 hp (75150 kW)
  • Large Articulated Tractors: 300600+ hp (225450+ kW)
Electric Tractors
  • Examples:
    • Monarch MK-V: ~70 hp (52 kW)
    • Solectrac e70N: ~70 hp (52 kW)
  • Battery Capacity: ~5070 kWh
  • Charging Power Needs: 1020 kW (Level 2 or 3)

2. Combine Harvesters

[1]

  • On average, 450 hp combine harvesters which harvest 50 ha/day use around 700L of fuel every day.[2]

3. Sprayers & Spreaders

Diesel Sprayers
  • Power Range: 150350 hp (110260 kW)
Electric Spray Units (Small-Scale)
  • Power: ~1030 kW
  • Use Case: Orchards, vineyards, precision tasks

4. Utility Vehicles (Farm UTVs/ATVs)

Electric UTVs
  • Power: 515 kW
  • Battery Capacity: 515 kWh
  • Use Case: Light hauling, inspection, fence work

5. Tillage Equipment

  • Tractor Power Required: 200500 hp (150370 kW)

Note:

  • Full-electric large farm machines are limited due to:
    • High continuous power demands
    • Long runtime needs
    • Rural charging infrastructure challenges
  • Battery swapping and hybrid tractors are emerging trends
